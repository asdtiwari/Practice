Scemantic & Non Scemantic Element

  • Scemantic elements are such elements which give information about the content or text to the programer and the browser, e.g., <table> </table>
    <header> </header>
    <nav> <nav>
    <section> </section> <aside> <aside>
    <article> </article>
    <footer> </footer>
  • Similarly Non-Scemantic elements are the elements which tell nothing about its content and their functionality, e.g. <span> </span>
